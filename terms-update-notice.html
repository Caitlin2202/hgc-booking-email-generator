Notice of Changes to Terms & Conditions
Effective 1st March 2026

Dear Members

We are writing to let you know about an update to our Terms and Conditions, which will take effect from 1st March 2026.

From time to time we review our Terms and Conditions to ensure they remain clear, up to date, and accurately reflect how the club operates. Following a recent review, we have updated our Terms and Conditions to provide greater clarity around our membership structure, programme delivery and payment arrangements.

The updated Terms and Conditions will apply to all memberships from 1st March. To help explain what this means in practice, we have summarised the key changes below.

  1. Membership fees are annual & spread monthly

    Membership fees are based on an annual programme and are spread across monthly payments for affordability.

    Monthly payments do not relate to a specific number of classes or training sessions in any individual month.

  2. Programme delivery across the year

    The club operates across the year in line with the programme schedule.

    The number of sessions delivered in any given month may vary due to holidays, competitions, events, or operational requirements.

    • Recreational and preschool memberships are based on a minimum number of sessions delivered across the year.
    • Competitive memberships are based on an annual training commitment equivalent to 48 weeks.
    • If external training or competitions for competitive members coincide with club sessions, these count as the session for that day; no additional sessions will be provided. Scheduled club sessions may be cancelled or adjusted if external training or competitions fall outside of normal training days.

  3. Missed sessions & non-attendance

    Membership fees remain payable regardless of attendance.

    Missed sessions do not entitle members to refunds, credits, or make-up sessions, including where absences are due to illness, injury, holiday, or other personal circumstances.

    Membership fees are non-refundable for non-attendance, except where the club is unable to deliver the programme in line with its stated commitments.

  4. Joining partway through the year

    Where a gymnast joins partway through the membership year, an adjustment payment may be required to ensure total payments reflect the cost of the remaining programme.

    This is separate from the standard monthly membership payments.

  5. Pay-as-you-go & stand-alone activities

    Some activities (such as workshops and camps) are booked and paid for separately and are subject to a 48-hour cancellation policy.

    Cancellations within 48 hours, or non-attendance, are non-refundable.

    The club may, at its discretion, allow changes to these bookings. These requests are subject to operational capacity and cannot be guaranteed.

  6. Withdrawals & notice periods

    The 4-week written notice requirement remains unchanged.

    Fees remain payable during the notice period, and classes cannot be transferred, refunded or credited once payment has been made, except where required by law.

    The club reserves the right to charge for any services already provided up to and including the notice period end, calculated pro-rata against the annual programme fee.

  7. Administrative fees

    The updated terms allow the club to charge reasonable administrative fees where additional work is required due to member actions (for example, repeated failed payments or excessive booking changes).

    Any such fees will be proportionate and communicated in advance.

  8. Exceptional circumstances

    The terms now clarify how extended or unplanned closures will be managed.

    Where closures result in a material reduction in the annual programme, the club will consider appropriate adjustments or alternative arrangements.

The full updated Terms and Conditions (effective 1st March) can be viewed here: https://hamiltongymnastics.clubright.co.uk/terms

If you are happy with these changes, no action is required.

If you do not wish to continue your membership under the updated Terms and Conditions, you may cancel your membership in line with the current terms by providing written notice to hgcenquiry@gmail.com before 1st March 2026. If you continue your membership on or after this date, this will constitute acceptance of the updated Terms and Conditions.

If you have any questions or would like to discuss these changes, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
